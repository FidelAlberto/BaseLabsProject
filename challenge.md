# Annie’s Magic Numbers  
**Discovering hard-to-find truths**

You are helping Annie, the owner of a large liquor and spirit distributor in the US. She wants to understand her profits and margins.

She provided the following link where you can download the relevant CSV data:  
https://www.pwc.com/us/en/careers/university-relations/data-and-analytics-case-studies-files.html

---

# Task at Hand

## 1. Data Ingestion
Efficiently ingest the relevant CSV files into a suitable database.

## 2. Data Transformation
Transform the data to calculate:
- Profit ($)
- Margin (%)

## 3. Reporting
Create a report for Annie outlining:

### Products
- Top 10 products with the highest profit ($)
- Top 10 products with the highest margin (%)

### Brands
- Top 10 brands with the highest profit ($)
- Top 10 brands with the highest margin (%)

### Loss-making items
- Identify brands and/or products that should be dropped as a wholesaler because they are losing money.
